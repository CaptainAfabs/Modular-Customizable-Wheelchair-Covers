# Bill of materials

Everything you need to make one full set (both wheels covered).

## Printed parts

| Part | Qty | Source |
|---|---|---|
| Spokeguard segment | 16 (8 per wheel) | [hardware/stl/Wheelchair-Spokeguard-Segment.stl](../hardware/stl/Wheelchair-Spokeguard-Segment.stl) |

Material: ~1 kg of PETG, ABS, ASA, or equivalent. See [PRINT_SETTINGS.md](PRINT_SETTINGS.md).

## Hardware

| Item | Qty per wheel | Qty for both | Spec | Approx cost (mid-2026 USD) |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Cable ties (zip ties) | ~30 | ~60 | **3 mm wide × 100 mm long**, nylon, black or any colour | $3–5 for a bag of 100 |
| Cable ties — spare | 10 | 20 | Same spec | included above |

Acceptable substitutes (the design works with any of these, with minor changes to routing):

- 2.5 mm × 100 mm ties — works fine, slightly easier to thread, holds about as well.
- 4.8 mm × 200 mm ties — works only on the wheel-attachment holes, not the seam apertures.
- Reusable silicone ties or hook-and-loop straps — for users who need to remove the cover often (e.g., before a flight).
- Wire ties / metal cable ties — for high-heat outdoor use.

## Tools

| Tool | Required? | Why |
|---|---|---|
| Flush cutters or sharp side cutters | **Required** | To trim tie tails close to the head so they don't snag clothing. |
| Pliers (standard) | Optional | Helps pull ties tight when you have a lot of friction. |
| Deburring tool or hobby knife | Optional | Clean up any stringing around aperture edges. |
| 4 mm drill bit (hand-twist) | Optional | Clear any aperture that printed slightly closed. |
| Sandpaper (200/400 grit) | Optional | Tune the tongue-and-groove fit if your printer runs tight. |
| Sharpie or paint pens | For customising | Add names, patterns, jersey numbers. |

## Optional: customisation supplies

- Acrylic spray paint (Krylon Fusion or Rust-Oleum 2X work well on PETG and ABS).
- Vinyl decals or stickers.
- Permanent markers (Sharpie Industrial holds up better outdoors than regular Sharpie).
- Acrylic paint pens for fine line work.

See [CUSTOMIZATION.md](CUSTOMIZATION.md) for ideas.
